Vulnerability Disclosure

Philosophy:
TNJ-Media believes effective disclosure of security vulnerabilities requires mutual trust, respect, transparency, and common good between TNJ-Media and security researchers.

Security Researchers

TNJ-Media accepts vulnerability reports from independent security researchers, industry partners, vendors, customers, and consultants.

Scope

This policy applies to any digital assets owned, operated, or maintained by TNJ-Media, including public websites.

Our Commitment to Researchers

  • Trust: Confidentiality in our exchanges with researchers.
  • Respect: Acknowledging contributions that keep our customers safe.
  • Transparency: Validating and remediating vulnerabilities efficiently.
  • Common Good: Ensuring security for those affected by vulnerabilities.
