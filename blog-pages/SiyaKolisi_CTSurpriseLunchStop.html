Lifestyle Siya Kolisi Turns Heads with Surprise Lunch Stop in Cape Town

June 22, 2025 ● Uhone Kwinda

Weekend Sports Guide 21-22 June 2025

Springbok captain Siya Kolisi caused a buzz in Cape Town this afternoon when he popped into a local eatery for a quick takeaway and fans couldn't get enough of it.

Kolisi stopped by New York Bagel, a popular spot in the eastern part of the city, and was met with cheers, applause, and even ululating from excited diners. One onlooker said, “People were so excited to see him. It was such a moment! He's so humble truly a hero.”

While waiting to place his order, Kolisi asked the crowd for suggestions. One person recommended the chicken-schnitzel bagel, and he went with that plus a bagel dog for good measure.

The Springboks are in town this week as they prepare for the much anticipated Qatar Airways Cup match on Saturday, where they'll take on the Barbarians for the first time ever on South African soil. With Kolisi in the city for training, fans might catch more unexpected sightings around Cape Town.

Just a day before his bagel stop, Kolisi posted TikTok videos from Johannesburg, where he was enjoying a traditional African meal with fellow Sharks player Aphelele Fassi at Sakhumzi restaurant in Zoo Lake. “I'm in Jozi. I needed some proper African food. I just want to go home trotters, veggies, and samp,” Kolisi says in the video. Fassi, for his part, was tucking into samp and tripe.

One fan commented: “You are everywhere, my Captain!”

Kolisi's ties to Cape Town go beyond rugby. His children live in the city with his ex-wife Rachel, and he's often spotted around town when visiting them. Even during the December holidays, while Rachel and the kids were overseas, Kolisi spent time in Cape Town fueling past rumours that he might move back to join the Stormers.

However, he remains firmly committed to the Sharks. Wherever he goes, one thing's clear: Siya Kolisi brings joy and pride to South Africans just by being himself.
