Why Media and Creative Must Work Together Again

These days, it can feel like AI is taking over everything and in the world of marketing, that's creating a lot of questions. What does this mean for creativity? Strategy? Jobs? It's normal to feel uncertain as technology changes how we work and live so quickly.Human ideas, emotions, and creativity are what bring brands to life. Technology is a tool but it's people who use it to connect, inspire, and innovate.

Growing Talent, Building Resilience

At the ACA, we see talent development as the heart of a strong, future ready industry. Supporting people at every stage of their careers isn't just the right thing to do it's how we keep our industry thriving in a fast changing world.That's why we're continuing to invest in meaningful education and development opportunities through a range of initiatives.

Introducing GenEra

Recently, we relaunched GenEra (previously known as FutureBallers). It's a space for young professionals in ACA member agencies to come together, learn from each other, and connect in a more relaxed, supportive environment.Through regular in person sessions, GenEra helps early career professionals navigate the real challenges of agency life with advice, mentorship, and plenty of shared experiences. Plus, GenEra participants get exclusive access to the school of thoughts, a learning hub for marketers to build skills, gain insights, and keep growing.

Supporting All Kinds of Talent

Our commitment doesn't stop with young professionals. We're also focused on making sure that all voices in our industry are heard and nurtured.That includes ongoing bursary programmes to help new talent break into the industry, and the continued success of our women in leadership programme now in its fifth round. By the end of this intake, 100 mid to senior level women will have had the chance to grow their leadership skills through mentorship and personal development.

Learning at Every Level

We've also introduced the Effie Academy, with learning opportunities for marketers at all stages of their careers. From Effie fundamentals (a flexible, on-demand course platform) to Effie College (hands on experience for marketing students), we're helping professionals stay sharp, relevant, and ready for what's next.

A Long-Term Commitment to People

Our belief is simple, when people have acces they gain confidence, when they feel part of a community they create better work. And when they are supported they stay and strive.At the ACA, we're playing the long game investing in talent, inclusion, and education so that our industry grows stronger, smarter, and more human, no matter how much the world changes.
