Sports Springboks vs Barbarians: How to Stay Warm and Show Your Team Spirit This Saturday

June 28, 2025 ● Uhone Kwinda

With the Springboks set to take on the Barbarians this Saturday at DHL Stadium in Cape Town, fans are buzzing with excitement. But along with the big game comes a chilly forecast that could put a damper on your match day plans if you're not prepared.

Earlier this week, the South African Weather Service warned of a strong cold front heading for Cape Town. Expect strong winds, heavy rain, and rough sea conditions. Saturday's weather? A high of just 15°C, cloudy skies, and showers throughout the day. So yes it's going to be cold and wet.But if there's one thing die-hard rugby fans know, it's that braving the weather is just part of the experience. Dressing right means you can stay warm, stay stylish, and still show off your Springboks pride.

Here's how to get game day ready.

1. Layer Smart

Start with a warm, fitted base layer like thermals or heat retaining tights. This keeps you warm without adding too much bulk.Over that, throw on your Springboks jersey it's the centerpiece of your outfit and a great conversation starter with fellow fans. Plus, nothing beats that green and gold on game day.

2. Choose the Right Jacket

A solid outer layer is a must. Go for a windbreaker or a puffer jacket, ideally in team colours or neutral tones that still complement your look. Look for something waterproof (or at least water resistant) to keep the rain out while you enjoy the game.

3. Don't Skip Accessories

3. Don't Skip Accessories

This is where you can really have some fun. A beanie, scarf, or gloves in green and gold or with your team's emblem adds both warmth and flair. Accessories are also an easy way to show support without covering your jersey.

4. Think Practical Shoes

You'll be on your feet a lot, so comfort is key. Pick shoes that can handle some rain, like ankle boots or sturdy trainers. Stick to darker colours or neutrals they're easier to clean and go with everything.

5. Bring the Energy

More than anything, your attitude is what makes the day. Smile, sing, chant, and high-five the fans around you. The cold won't feel nearly as bad when you're caught up in the excitement of the game.And hey those match day photos in your cozy, spirited outfit? They'll be keepsakes long after the final whistle.

So gear up, dress smart, and bring the heat even if the weather doesn't. Go Bokke!
