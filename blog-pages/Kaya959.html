Media Kaya 959 Leads the Pack in Online Radio Engagement

June 19, 2025 ● Uhone Kwinda

Kaya 959 has been leading the pack online, holding the number one spot for average daily active users among commercial radio station websites in South Africa for five straight months from January to May 2025.

Verified audience data shows that www.kaya959.co.za consistently pulled in more users each day than major competitors like Jacaranda FM, East Coast Radio, and platforms under Primedia Broadcasting. February marked a high point with over 50,500 users in that month alone, and even in other months, the site kept a strong pace, averaging just over 46,000 users monthly.

Recently, the station took its presence offline and into the community with the In Your City Roadshow, held at The Forum in Bryanston and at the UCT Graduate Business School in Cape Town, the events were done in partnership with Kaya's new sales partner, United Stations. At these gatherings, they shared findings from the 2024 BrandMapp Market Research Survey, which helped solidify Kaya 959's strong influence in Gauteng's urban market.

Managing Director Colleen Louw emphasized the deep trust that Kaya's audience has in the station, noting that listeners rely on them for music, news, and a sense of companionship. She highlighted that Kaya 959 reaches the largest black commercial radio audience in greater Johannesburg a group that's both highly engaged and valuable to advertisers. With their growing digital footprint, Kaya is offering more ways than ever to connect whether on air, online, or at events in the community.

The BrandMapp research also revealed several standout insights. For one, Kaya dominates the 25-49 age group in Gauteng, with 68% of its local audience tuning in daily. Listeners also place a high level of trust in FM radio more than TV, social media, or other digital platforms when it comes to news. Mobile engagement is rising too, with 31% of respondents saying they consume Kaya's content via their phones every day.

Part of the station's pull comes from its popular personalities people like Sizwe Dhlomo, Thomas Msengana, Andy Magondwana, Glen Lewis, Gugu Mfuphi, and Brenda Sisane, who all keep listeners coming back. Social media is another strong suit, with Kaya's audience especially active on platforms like LinkedIn, TikTok, and X. Musically, Kaya 959 keeps things broad and inclusive, with a mix of R&B, soul, gospel, Afro-jazz and more reflecting the depth and diversity of its community.

All of this reinforces what Kaya 959 has become: not just a radio station, but a trusted, multi platform brand that speaks authentically to its audience.
