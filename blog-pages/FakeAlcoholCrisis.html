Media Fake Alcohol Crisis in South Africa

June 12, 2025 ● Uhone Kwinda

South Africa is facing a dangerous rise in fake and illegal alcohol, with over R20 billion worth found in stores, nightclubs, restaurants, and even trusted liquor outlets. This comes after research by the Alcoholic Beverages Association of South Africa, showing that fake alcohol is making people sick and in some cases, it's deadly.

What's worse is that many consumers don't even know they're drinking fake booze. It's packaged to look real but contains toxic, harmful chemicals. And so far, there's no clear plan from the government on how they'll tackle the issue

That's why now, more than ever, it's important to choose safe.

One reliable and healthy option, Wine Wine grapes are a special kind of grape used to make wine. They're smaller, sweeter, and have thicker skins than the grapes you usually eat.

How is wine better than other alcohol?

When enjoyed in moderation, wine especially red wine can have some health benefits. It has natural antioxidants that may be good for your heart.

Wine also has less alcohol than spirits like whiskey or vodka, which can be easier on your body if you drink responsibly. Plus, it pairs nicely with food and is often part of a more relaxed, social experience.
